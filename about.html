About

Exonemo is a Japanese artist group which is composed of the duo Kensuke Sembo and Yae Akaiwa. They started their careers anonymously in 1996, and had enjoyed immersing themselves in music and alternative cultures which had impacted and influenced their net art practice. Due to owning only one computer, their collaboration was a necessity, ultimately leading to the formation of their group known as Exonemo.
